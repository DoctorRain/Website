About Me

Hi, My name is Cheong Hou Weng (張皓榮), you can also call me Rain or Rain Cheong.

I was born in Macau on 16th December 2004, currently living in Macau and studying at Colégio Diocesano de São José (5ª), before that I had studied at Macau Anglican College and graduated.

I'm a multilingual person, whom proficient in three languages - English, Cantonese, and Mandarin, with Cantonese being my mother tougue. I have learned English and Mandarin in my early age as a primary student, which is also the reasons that encourage me to learn more languages.

At the moment, I'm interested at Japanese and Portuguese. My interest can traced back to seven years ago, since then, I have been continuously studying these languages. As a result of my hard work, I had become proficient enough in these languages to understand what foreigners are saying.
